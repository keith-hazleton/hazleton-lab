About

Keith Hazleton, MD, PhD

I am an Attending Physician in the Division of Gastroenterology, Hepatology, and Nutrition at Children's Hospital Los Angeles. My research focuses on the intersections of nutrition, infection, and liver disease in pediatric populations.

My work investigates how dietary factors influence disease susceptibility and outcomes, with particular emphasis on Clostridioides difficile infection, portal hypertension, and the gut-liver axis. I collaborate with the Lozupone Lab at the University of Colorado Anschutz Medical Campus to integrate advanced microbiome analysis methods into my research.
