About

Keith Hazleton, MD, PhD

Dr. Hazleton is an Attending Physician in the Division of Gastroenterology, Hepatology, and Nutrition at Children's Hospital Los Angeles. His research focuses on the intersections of nutrition, infection, and liver disease in pediatric populations.

His work investigates how dietary factors influence disease susceptibility and outcomes, with particular emphasis on Clostridioides difficile infection, portal hypertension, and the gut-liver axis. He collaborates with the Lozupone Lab at the University of Colorado Anschutz Medical Campus to integrate advanced microbiome analysis methods into his research.

Research Interests

  • Dietary influences on C. difficile infection
  • Portal hypertension management
  • Microbiome-liver disease interactions
  • Nutrition-driven illness in pediatric gastroenterology
  • Gut-liver axis
